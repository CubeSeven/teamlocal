La Bella Casa is a family-run joinery on Skiathos that has been making kitchens, wardrobes, pergolas and custom wooden, aluminium and PVC windows and doors since 1986. Work is carried out to order and the team handles both new-build projects and renovations across the island.

La Bella Casa is a proud sponsor of Skiathos Atletico, supporting the local football community on the island.
