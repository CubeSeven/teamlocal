Supway offers guided stand-up paddleboard and kayak tours around Skiathos, run by a certified local guide. Tours cover the Sklithri to Kanapitsa coastline, hidden coves around the island, and golden-hour sunset paddles. Equipment is provided and tours welcome paddlers aged 14 and up, with no prior experience required.

Supway is a proud sponsor of Skiathos Atletico, supporting the local football community on the island.
