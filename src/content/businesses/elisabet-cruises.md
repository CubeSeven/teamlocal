Elisabet Cruises runs a fleet of cruises across the Northern Sporades, with set departures from Skiathos and from Amaliapolis & Achillio on the mainland. Routes include the Skopelos & Alonnisos cruise, the Mamma Mia filming-locations tour, sunset cruises and full-day Pagasetic Gulf trips. Private and event charters can be arranged on request.

Elisabet Cruises is a proud sponsor of Skiathos Atletico, supporting the local football community on the island.
