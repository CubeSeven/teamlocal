Areti is a small cluster of studios and apartments on the western side of Skiathos, a short walk from the beach. Rooms come with kitchenettes, TV and sea-view balconies; options include studios for two, one-bedroom apartments and larger maisonettes. The pool area has a bar serving snacks, salads and burgers, and hosts a weekly Greek-BBQ night.

Areti Studios and Apartments is a proud sponsor of Skiathos Atletico, supporting the local football community on the island.
