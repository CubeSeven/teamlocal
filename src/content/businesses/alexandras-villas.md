Alexandras Villas has been welcoming visitors to Skiathos since 1980, when the family first began hosting guests. The property sits in the middle of a 4,500 sqm olive garden in Agia Paraskevi, about three minutes on foot from the award-winning Agia Paraskevi beach and a 15-minute drive from Skiathos Town. Each self-catering villa is around 50 sqm with a full kitchen, terrace and views of the pine-covered hills.

Alexandras Villas is a proud sponsor of Skiathos Atletico, supporting the local football community on the island.
