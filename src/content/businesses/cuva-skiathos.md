Cuva sits on one of the most photographed squares in Skiathos Town, with open-air tables, string lights and a relaxed Greek-island atmosphere. The kitchen focuses on classic Greek recipes prepared to order, and the evenings regularly feature live Greek nights with music and dancing.

Cuva Greek Cuisine is a proud sponsor of Skiathos Atletico, supporting the local football community on the island.
