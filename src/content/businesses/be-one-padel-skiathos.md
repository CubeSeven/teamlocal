Be-One is a padel and social club on the hill above Skiathos, set in an olive garden with views of the pine-covered mountains. Facilities include outdoor padel courts, equipment hire, group and private lessons for kids and adults, and one-to-one personal training sessions. Memberships and drop-in court bookings are available.

Be-One Padel Skiathos is a proud sponsor of Skiathos Atletico, supporting the local football community on the island.
