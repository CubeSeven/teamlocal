Villas Phos is a 240 sqm private villa on Skiathos made up of two independent apartments — a ground-floor apartment with two bedrooms and a three-storey maisonette — making it well suited to large families or groups travelling together. Outside, an infinity pool looks out over the Aegean, with shaded dining areas and a landscaped garden. Wi-Fi, air-conditioning, full kitchen and parking are included.

Villas Phos is a proud sponsor of Skiathos Atletico, supporting the local football community on the island.
