Dough Hook is a family-run bakehouse and patisserie on the Megali Ammos seafront. The menu covers traditional Greek pies, buttery patisseries, breakfast sandwiches, single-origin coffee and handcrafted custom cakes for birthdays and events. Everything is baked fresh from the oven each morning.

Dough Hook Bakehouse & Patisserie is a proud sponsor of Skiathos Atletico, supporting the local football community on the island.
